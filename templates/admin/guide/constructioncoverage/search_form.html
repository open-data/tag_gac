Instructions

For most entities most construction is covered by TAs.

For the Department of Transport construction is not covered.


There is a row for each organization. You can edit the models here or by clicking the row ID.

There are checkboxes on the far left. If you want to delete a row, click the checkbox then under Action select the dropdown option and click Go.

ORG FK: This is the name of the entity. Click on this to edit the information in a row

The rest of the columns are for Canada's trade agreements.

A checkmark indicates that for that TA and entity, construction is not covered.

No checkmark indicates that for that TA and entity, the usual construction covered applies.

Entities for which all the usual construction coverage always applies do not need to be listed here.
