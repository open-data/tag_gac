Instructions

Each trade agreement contains a schedule or entities that are either covered by the trade agreement

or not covered by the trade agreement. The names of these entities are in the list below, some of the

may be different then what is in the trade agreement because this list uses the applied titles.


There is a row for each organization. You can edit the models here or by clicking the row ID.

Use the search field to search by entity name. Or you can filter entities by trade agreement coverage.

There are checkboxes on the far left. If you want to delete a row, click the checkbox then under Action select the dropdown option and click Go.


ID: Click on the row ID to edit the information in a row.

ENTITIES [EN]: The name of the entity in english.

ENTITIES [FR]: The name of the entity in french.

The rest of the columns are for Canada's trade agreements.

A checkmark indicates that the entity is covered by the trade agreement. No checkmark means the entity is not covered by the trade agreement.


The data used to populate the database can be found in the following documents:
