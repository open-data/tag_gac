Instructions

Some agreements have excluded certain commodity codes only for certain departments.

For instance, in CCFTA, management of government owned facilities by DND, TC, and NRCan

is not covered by the TA. However, for CKFTA the management of government owned facilities

is not covered for all entities. In that case the coverage goes in the Commodity Codes section.


There is a row for each combination of Commodity Code & Organization.

Use the search field to search by organization of Commodity Code. Or you can filter entities by

trade agreement coverage.

There are checkboxes on the far left. If you want to delete a row, click the checkbox then under

select the dropdown option and click Go.


CODE ORGANIZATION EXCLUSION: Click on the text to edit the information in a row.

The rest of the columns are for Canada's trade agreements.

A checkmark indicates that the combination of organization and commodity code applies to that TA.
