Instructions

For most entities all goods are covered by the trade agreements.

Goods coverage refers to those entities for which the coverage of goods is limited.

This is usually National Defense, RCMP, and the Canadian Coast Guard.


There is a row for each organization. You can edit the models here or by clicking the row ID.

Use the search field to search by entity name. Or you can filter entities by trade agreement coverage.

There are checkboxes on the far left. If you want to delete a row, click the checkbox then under Action select the dropdown option and click Go.

ORG FK: This is the name of the entity. Click on this to edit the information in a row

The rest of the columns are for Canada's trade agreements.

A checkmark indicates that for that TA and entity, there is a limited goods coverage.

No checkmark indicates that for that TA and entity, all goods are covered..

Entities for which all goods are always covered in all TA do not need to be listed here.


The data used to populate the database can be found in the following documents:
