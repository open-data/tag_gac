Instructions

Each trade agreement contains a table with monetary threshold, above which the trade agreement applies.

Every two years, the thresholds in the free trade agreements are updated.

Check Policy Notices and TB Circulars - Contracting for the current thresholds.


There is a row for each commodity type. You can edit the models here or by clicking the comodity type.

You can filter by commodity type.

There are checkboxes on the far left. If you want to delete a row, click the checkbox then under Action select the dropdown option and click Go.


Commodity Type: One of goods, services or construction.

The rest of the columns are for Canada's trade agreements.

The value indicates the current trade agreement thresholds for each agreement.
